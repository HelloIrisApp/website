Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2025

Iris is provided by Amore & Industrial LLC ("we", "us", or "our"), a software company incorporated in Delaware, USA. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Iris application and related services (the "Service"). Privacy isn't a feature — it's a foundation. Iris works quietly in the background of your life, and we take the trust that requires very seriously.

1. Information we collect

We collect the minimum necessary to make Iris genuinely useful:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and time zone when you register.
  • Calendar data — events, schedules, and metadata from calendars you connect (Google, Apple, Microsoft). We access only what's needed to serve you.
  • Contacts — when you grant access, we read names, email addresses, and relevant metadata from your device or platform contacts (Google Contacts, Apple Contacts, or Microsoft People). This allows Iris to suggest attendees, resolve names when planning events, and help coordinate with the people in your life. We do not store your full contacts list — we access contact data on demand, only when needed to fulfill a specific action.
  • Forwarded emails — when you choose to forward emails to Iris, we process those messages to take action on your behalf. We do not access your inbox directly.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the Service, to help us improve it over time.

2. How we use your information

We use your information to provide and personalize the Service, deliver your daily brief and calendar intelligence, communicate with you about your account, and keep things secure. We do not use your information for advertising, and we will never sell your data.

3. Third-party connections

When you connect Iris to third-party platforms, those connections are governed by each platform's own privacy policy. We handle your data with the same care regardless of source. We never transfer your data to third parties for advertising, resale, credit determination, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and understand how the Service is used. No advertising or tracking cookies. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard security measures — encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, regular audits. No internet service can guarantee absolute security, and we encourage strong, unique passwords.

6. Your rights & access

You may access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through account settings. To request complete deletion, contact us below — we'll process it within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

Iris is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

8. Data transfer

Your information may be stored or processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
